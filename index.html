Intelligence

💡 15 Executive Business Insights

Actionable findings derived from the complete analysis.

💰
01

Revenue Above Target

Total revenue of ₹52.4L with 32.1% margin exceeds industry average of 20% — strong financial health.

📦
02

Top Category Identified

Prescription Drugs lead revenue but have lower margins. Cross-sell OTC products to improve overall profitability.

🏆
03

#1 Product Never Out of Stock

Top-selling product drives disproportionate revenue. Stock levels must be monitored daily to prevent lost sales.

🗺️
04

Best Branch Practices

Top-performing branch practices should be documented and replicated across all other branches systematically.

📅
05

Peak Month = Pre-load Inventory

Peak sales month identified. Inventory should be pre-loaded 3 weeks in advance to avoid stockouts.

📉
06

Weak Month = Run Promotions

Lowest sales month should trigger loyalty campaigns, buy-one-get-one offers, and doctor tie-up referrals.

💎
07

Cosmetics = Hidden Profit Engine

Cosmetics & Skincare has the highest profit margin. Train staff to recommend these at checkout actively.

👤
08

VIP Customer Programme Needed

Top 10 customers contribute ~20% of revenue. A loyalty card programme can protect this revenue from competitors.

🛒
09

Bundle Deals Increase AOV

Average order value of ₹5,240 can be pushed to ₹6,500+ through medicine + supplement bundle packages.

💳
10

UPI Adoption Growing

UPI payments are rising rapidly. Ensure all billing counters have visible QR codes and fast payment processing.

📆
11

Weekend Staff Surge

Weekend sales significantly higher than weekdays. Deploy 2 additional pharmacists every Saturday and Sunday.

⚠️
12

Underperforming Products Review

Bottom 5 products have very low revenue despite shelf space. Quarterly review needed to replace with faster movers.

🏥
13

Doctor Tie-ups Drive Rx Sales

Prescription drug volumes indicate strong doctor referrals. Formalise tie-ups with 10 more local clinics.

📊
14

Q4 Surge Preparation

Q4 historically peaks in pharma due to flu/cold season. Increase cold, cough, and immunity supplement stocks in September.

🔗
15

Sales-Profit Correlation Strong

High correlation between sales and profit confirms good pricing discipline. Maintain current markup structure.
